Setup Guide

Two things to set before launch. (1) The destination link points to a placeholder quantiswm.com/training — swap in the real webinar registration / landing page URL (e.g. the Zoom, Demio, or LeadPages sign-up). (2) Quantis is an SEC/state-registered investment adviser — run all copy past your compliance/RIA review before publishing. Avoid performance guarantees, keep claims substantiated, and include any required adviser disclosures on the landing page.

Campaign Setup — Step by Step

  1. Open Ads Manager → click Create → choose Manual setup.
  2. Select the Leads objective for the registration push (or Sales if optimizing for a registration Pixel event on the landing page). Create one campaign per funnel stage so budgets and bids stay isolated.
  3. Name your campaign using the convention: Quantis_Launch001_{Stage}_{Topic} (e.g. Quantis_Launch001_Cold_Income).
  4. Enable Advantage+ campaign budget with a starting daily budget of $30–$75 per stage. Increase by 20% every 3 days once cost-per-registration stabilizes.
  5. Ad set: set conversion location to Website (or Instant Forms for lead-form sign-ups), conversion event to Lead / CompleteRegistration, attribution to 7-day click + 1-day view.
  6. Audience: apply the targeting profile for that funnel stage (see "Audience Targeting" panel).
  7. Placements: use Advantage+ Placements but exclude Audience Network for cold campaigns to protect brand quality.
  8. Ad creative: upload the corresponding PNG, paste Primary Text, Headline, Link Description, set CTA to Sign Up, and paste the destination link with UTM parameters from the cards above.
  9. Pixel events: verify the Lead / CompleteRegistration event fires on the webinar thank-you page.
  10. Publish → let each ad set run a minimum of 7 days or until it hits 50 registrations before judging performance.

Cold · Ad 01 — Outliving Savings

Broad pre-retiree. Northern Virginia / DC / Maryland (DMV), ages 55–67, all genders. Interests: retirement planning, 401(k), IRA, Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab. Layer income-proxy behaviors (frequent travelers, premium brands). Exclude existing clients & past-30-day site visitors.

Consideration · Ad 02 — Market Risk

Engaged investors. DMV, 55–70. Interests: stock market, investing, CNBC, Wall Street Journal, Morningstar, market volatility. Layer 1–2% Lookalike of past webinar registrants. Exclude existing clients.

Mid-Funnel · Ad 03 — Taxes

Higher-asset, tax-sensitive. DMV, 58–72. Interests: tax planning, estate planning, RMDs, Social Security, business owners, Forbes. Behaviors: likely $250k+ HH income / investable-asset proxies. Layer Lookalike of high-value clients.

Bottom-Funnel · Ad 04 — Inflation

Warm retargeting. Past-30-day site & landing-page visitors who didn't register, video viewers (50%+) of Ads 01–03, engaged FB/IG followers last 30 days, and uploaded email/CRM lists. Cap frequency at ~2/week.

UTM Parameter Templates

Paste the snippet below into the URL Parameters field in Ads Manager (under "Tracking" in the ad-level setup). Meta will auto-populate {{campaign.name}}, {{adset.name}}, and {{ad.name}} per impression — no manual editing required.

Universal Meta tracking string (recommended)

utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign={{campaign.name}}&utm_content={{ad.name}}&utm_term={{adset.name}}&utm_id={{campaign.id}}

Naming convention reference

  • utm_content: ad{number}_{painpoint}
