Questions

Before you register

Why did I receive this invitation?

This session was built for a specific group: savers age 50 and up with a real retirement balance in a 401(k), IRA, 403(b), or TSP who want to understand how physical gold fits in. If you are here, you fit that profile. Seats are kept limited so the room stays small enough for live Q&A.

Can I really move my 401(k) or IRA into gold without taxes or penalties?

Yes. Done as a direct, trustee-to-trustee transfer, the money moves from one custodian to the other without you ever touching it, so no taxes and no penalties apply. We walk through exactly how it works, step by step, so the fear of a tax mistake never holds you back.

I already work with a financial advisor. Is this still worth my time?

Especially then. Most advisors never mention that physical gold can sit inside a retirement account at all. This session simply shows you the option, in plain English, so you can raise it with your advisor and decide for yourself whether it belongs in your plan.

What if my 401(k) is with my current employer?

It depends on the plan. An old or former-employer 401(k), an IRA, or a TSP can usually be rolled over at any time. A 401(k) with your current employer often requires that you be 59 1/2 or older, or have left that employer, though some plans allow in-service rollovers. We help you figure out your specific situation, with no obligation.

What if I just keep doing what I'm doing?

Probably nothing dramatic this week. But every year fully in paper is another year exposed to the exact thing you are worried about, with none of the protection you would have chosen if you had known your options. The point of this workshop is to show you that choice while there is still time to use it.

Should I invite my spouse?

Please do. These are joint decisions, and the couples who get the most out of it attend together, so they leave on the same page.

What if I can't attend live?

Register anyway. We send a recording to everyone who reserves a seat, so you can watch whenever it is convenient. The live Q&A, though, only happens once.

What happens at the end?

You will get the full picture, yours to use however you like. If you would like help applying it to your own accounts, you will have the option to book a no-obligation 15-minute consultation. Either way, you walk away ahead.
