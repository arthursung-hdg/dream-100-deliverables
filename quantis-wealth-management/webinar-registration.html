Is This For You?

Who this training is for (and who it isn't)

We kept this session focused, so it lands hardest for the right people. See where you fit before you reserve a seat.

This is for you if…

  • ✓You are 55 or older and within about ten years of retirement, or recently retired.
  • ✓You have built a real nest egg ([$1M+] saved) with more than one moving part to coordinate.
  • ✓You are juggling a separate advisor, CPA, and maybe an estate attorney who never talk to each other.
  • ✓You want one coordinated plan for income, taxes, and legacy, not another product pitch.
  • ✓You live in Northern Virginia or the greater Washington DC area.

This is NOT for you if…

  • ✕You are decades from retirement and just starting to save.

  • ✕You just want someone to sell you a single product and move on.

Questions

Before you register

Why did I receive this invitation?

This session was built for a specific group: Northern Virginia retirees and pre-retirees with a real nest egg and more than one moving part to coordinate. If you are here, you fit that profile. Seats are kept limited so the room stays small enough for live Q&A.

Who is this for?

Retirees and pre-retirees (roughly ages 55 and up) in the Northern Virginia and DC area who want a clearer, more coordinated plan for income, taxes, and legacy.

I already work with a financial advisor. Is this still worth my time?

Especially then. Most people have an advisor for investments, a separate CPA for taxes, and maybe an attorney for the estate, and no one connects them. This session shows you what a coordinated plan looks like, so you can judge for yourself whether anything is slipping through the cracks in yours.

What happens if I just keep doing what I'm doing?

Probably nothing dramatic this week. But uncoordinated withdrawals, missed tax windows, and drifting estate documents compound quietly, year after year. The point of this session is to show you those leaks while there is still time to fix them.

Should I invite my spouse?

Please do. These are joint decisions, and the couples who get the most out of it attend together, so they leave on the same page.

What if I can't attend live?

Register anyway. We will send a recording to everyone who signs up, so you can watch whenever it is convenient. The live Q&A, though, only happens once.

How long is it?

About 45 minutes of training, plus live Q&A at the end.

What happens at the end?

You will get the full framework, yours to use however you like. If you would like help applying it to your own numbers, you will have the option to book a working session with the team. Either way, you walk away ahead.
