Step 2 · Registration Page

Get people to say "yes"

When people don't know you, they couldn't care less about what you do.

That's why we lead with value first: an invitation to a 60–90 minute live webinar.

The Webinar Funnel

  • Invites people to free, valuable training. An easy yes.
  • Low-commitment, so far more people opt in.
  • They show up ready to learn, not with their sales guard up.
  • Education-led: we teach, they convince themselves.
  • Booking a call with you at the end of the webinar feels like the natural next step.

Book-a-Call or Video Sales Letter Funnel

  • "Book a call" when they don't even know you. Hard pass.
  • High-commitment, so the cost per meeting is $400+.
  • You repeat the same pitch to a skeptical and uninformed lead.
  • Hard-sell: pitch, handle objections, push for the close.
  • They feel pressured and get their sales guard up.
