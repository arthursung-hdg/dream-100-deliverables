Is This For You?

Who this training is for (and who it isn't)

This is for you if…

  • ✓You've been careful with money for forty years, but if someone asked how much you can safely spend each year in retirement, you'd honestly be guessing.
  • ✓Most of your savings sits in a 401(k) or IRA, and you've never really reckoned with how much of it is actually the IRS's, or what happens when forced withdrawals begin at 73.
  • ✓Your last paycheck is in sight, and the silence after it worries you, because no one has ever shown you how to turn a pile of accounts into a steady monthly income.
  • ✓Your financial advisor and your CPA have never once spoken, and you're the only person sitting in the middle trying to make their advice add up.
  • ✓You're a Northern Virginia, DC, or DMV household within about five years of retirement, with $2 million or more saved, who would rather enjoy your wealth than spend your sixties managing it.

This is NOT for you if…

  • ✕You're decades from retirement and just starting to save. This training is built for people whose retirement decisions are imminent and expensive.
  • ✕You know you should have a real plan, but you're not actually willing to act on what you learn, even once you see the value in it.
  • ✕You're hunting for a hot stock tip, a secret tax loophole, or a get-rich-quick shortcut. This is careful planning, not magic.
  • ✕You just want someone to manage investments, and you'd rather not look at how your taxes, income, and estate all fit together.
